# TheTidbit Hero Banner Image Generation Prompts

## Master Prompt Template

## PROMPT 1: Round Sling Bag (Sling Bag Rounded)

```
Create a premium hero banner image for an e-commerce website selling handmade jute bags. This image will be used for both desktop and mobile displays.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (landscape)
- Resolution: 2560x1440 pixels minimum (4K quality preferred: 3840x2160)
- Format: High-quality PNG or JPG
- Color Space: sRGB
- File Size: Optimized but high quality

COMPOSITION & LAYOUT (CRITICAL):
- LEFT 35-40% OF FRAME: "TEXT ZONE" - Keep completely EMPTY or with minimal, simple background
  * Solid warm-toned wall (terracotta, beige, or cream)
  * OR subtle textured surface
  * NO competing visual elements, patterns, or objects
  * This space is reserved for text overlay (headline, CTA, etc.)
  
- RIGHT 60-65% OF FRAME: "PRODUCT ZONE" - Product and model positioned here
  * Round red jute sling bag with intricate mandala embroidery is the HERO ELEMENT
  * Product should be LARGEST and SHARPEST element in frame
  * All embroidery details, colors, and textures must be crystal clear
  * Bag should be prominently displayed and unobstructed

PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:
- Round red jute sling bag with colorful mandala-like embroidery pattern
- Bag must be in SHARP FOCUS - every detail visible
- Showcase the intricate embroidery: multi-colored circles, geometric patterns
- Bag should appear premium, handcrafted, and well-made
- Position bag so it's clearly visible: either being worn crossbody, held, or placed prominently
- NO text, logos, or overlays on the bag itself
- Bag should be well-lit to show all colors and textures

MODEL & POSITIONING:
- Young Indian woman (age 22-28) positioned on the RIGHT side of frame
- Model should be in CENTER-RIGHT or RIGHT area (not left)
- Options for positioning:
  * Standing, wearing bag crossbody across her body
  * Sitting on simple wooden chair/stool, bag on lap or beside her
  * Standing, holding bag in hand showing it to camera
- Model should look directly at camera with warm, confident, natural smile
- Model's clothing: Simple, elegant - white kurti, or pastel-colored top, or boho-style dress
- Model should complement but NOT dominate - product is the hero
- Model's face and expression should be friendly and approachable

BACKGROUND & SETTING:
- Simple, warm-toned background that doesn't compete with product
- Options:
  * Textured reddish-brown or terracotta wall (like Zouk style)
  * Soft beige or cream-colored wall
  * Minimal indoor setting with warm lighting
- Background should be slightly OUT OF FOCUS (bokeh effect) to emphasize product
- Avoid busy patterns, cluttered scenes, or distracting elements
- Keep it clean, minimal, and premium-looking

LIGHTING:
- Natural, warm lighting (golden hour feel)
- Soft, diffused light that highlights the bag's colors and details
- No harsh shadows on the product
- Product should be well-lit and vibrant
- Background can be slightly darker to create depth

COLOR PALETTE:
- Warm, earthy tones (browns, beiges, terracotta)
- Product colors should POP (red bag with colorful embroidery)
- Overall warm, inviting, premium aesthetic
- Colors should complement jute material (natural, earthy feel)

STYLE & AESTHETIC:
- Premium Indian D2C brand aesthetic (like Zouk, Fabindia)
- Modern yet traditional
- Clean, uncluttered composition
- Product-first approach
- Handcrafted, artisanal feel
- Sustainable, eco-friendly vibe

PSYCHOLOGY & VISUAL HIERARCHY:
- LEFT SIDE (empty): Where user's eye STARTS - builds trust with text
- RIGHT SIDE (product): Where user's eye ENDS - visual reward after reading
- Natural reading flow: Text → Product → CTA
- Product is the "reward" after understanding the value proposition

QUALITY REQUIREMENTS:
- Professional product photography quality
- Sharp focus on product (bag should be razor-sharp)
- Soft focus on background (bokeh effect)
- High dynamic range
- No compression artifacts
- Ready for web use but high quality

CROPPING CONSIDERATIONS:
- Image should work when cropped to different aspect ratios:
  * Desktop: 16:9 (full width)
  * Mobile: Can be cropped to 9:16 or 4:5 if needed
- Important elements (product) should remain visible in all crops
- Left text zone should always be available for overlay

FINAL CHECKLIST:
✓ Left 35-40% is empty/simple (text zone)
✓ Product is on right 60-65% (product zone)
✓ Product is sharp, clear, and prominently displayed
✓ All embroidery details are visible
✓ Model is positioned on right side
✓ Background is simple and doesn't compete
✓ Warm, premium aesthetic
✓ High resolution (2560x1440 minimum)
✓ Works for both desktop and mobile
```

## Quick Reference: Key Requirements

### MUST HAVE:
1. **Left 35-40% EMPTY** - Text zone, no competing elements
2. **Right 60-65% PRODUCT** - Sharp, clear, prominent
3. **High Resolution** - 2560x1440 minimum, 4K preferred
4. **Product in Focus** - Razor-sharp, all details visible
5. **Simple Background** - Warm tones, out of focus
6. **Model on Right** - Supporting role, not dominant
7. **16:9 Aspect Ratio** - Works for desktop and mobile

### MUST NOT HAVE:
1. ❌ Text or logos on the bag
2. ❌ Busy patterns in left zone
3. ❌ Product on left side
4. ❌ Cluttered backgrounds
5. ❌ Low resolution
6. ❌ Blurry product
7. ❌ Competing visual elements in text zone

## Psychology Behind This Strategy

- **Left Side (Text Zone)**: Where user's eye starts → Builds trust and context
- **Right Side (Product Zone)**: Where user's eye ends → Visual reward and proof
- **Natural Flow**: Text → Product → CTA = Information → Validation → Action
- **Product Visibility**: Never obscured, always the hero element
- **Trust Building**: Text first, product second = Considered purchase flow

This strategy is proven by successful D2C brands like Zouk and works perfectly for TheTidbit's premium positioning.
